✨ Story Summary

In this comic sci-fi adventure, heroes Capt. SUPER and DUPER launch from the dying planet CARNIVOSIA in search of a mythical crystal — the only hope to restore their world.

After a meteor crash-lands them on the wrong spot, they team up with the eccentric DR. BLOOPNAR and the hilariously unreliable device SIMETRA. Their journey takes them through Cyberpunk City, a glowing forest trap, and into the Crystal Cave.

Along the way, they befriend a dramatic robot, survive betrayal, and discover that the crystal was buried under their crashed UFO ARTEMIS all along!

A story celebrating friendship, forgiveness, and teamwork.

📖 Detailed Story

1

The Crash Landing

Capt. SUPER and DUPER set out to find the mythical crystal believed to be the only way to restore their collapsing planet. After receiving a map, the team launches with confidence but is immediately struck by a meteor and crash-lands on the right planet, but in the wrong place.

As they regroup, a suspicious figure secretly steals an important gear from their ship, leaving them unable to continue. While taking stock of their broken UFO ARTEMIS, they encounter the eccentric "DR. BLOOPNAR, THE INTERGALACTIC JELLYBEAN" and a quirky device called "SIMETRA" that tries in vain to help but keeps malfunctioning each time.

CAPT. SUPER studies the map, spots a nearby village, and leads the group onward in hopes of finding help.

2

Cyberpunk City

They arrive into the striking, futuristic setting of CYBERPUNK CITY, where their search for the missing gear takes an unexpected turn. As they marvel at the towering, colorful city, they encounter a dramatic and witty robot they call a "Mechanical Frankenstein" who reveals that the gear they need has damaged her life system.

The heroes recover the gear — their first missing UFO piece — while DR. BLOOPNAR repairs the robot, leading to growing trust. Although offended by the "Frankenstein" insult, the robot agrees to help them find the crystal, joining as a new ally.

3

The Robot's Betrayal

Now a group of 4, they reach a glowing forest, only to discover that their new robot companion has secretly been plotting against them. Pretending to help, the robot lures the group into a trap and freezes them with the push of a button, creating a humorous interruption in the form of an absurd advertisement for CHEESE!

When her plan to steal their UFO fails, the team uses SIMETRA to get directions to the crystal caves — though the clue it shoots out is a picture of Shrek! Despite the confusion and comedy, the group finds their way forward.

4

The Crystal Cave

In the Crystal Cave, the heroes finally discover the last missing gear. Their relief is interrupted when the robot appears — the cave was her hidden base all along!

In a surprisingly emotional moment, the robot raps and beatboxes her story:

"I've been robbing from spaceships to get back home
'Coz my people left me here, a long time ago
I've been using this cave as a base, you know!
But now that you caught me, I don't know where to go!"

Instead of rejecting her, the heroes offer a second chance — if she promises to help with honesty.

5

The Triumphant Return

The heroes return to the crash site, repair their UFO, and in a comic twist discover that the crystal had been buried below their crashed UFO ARTEMIS all along!

With the crystal in hand, they head back to CARNIVOSIA, where they are celebrated with promotions and certificates. Their leaders welcome the new companions. SIMETRA attempts to translate and delivers: "Which is more pathetic? The tin can that can't pass an I'm-not-a-robot test, or the guy who thinks a lab coat makes him intimidating?"

The robot's parting promise: "Thank you for forgiving me and teaching me the value of friendship. I promise I will be truthful next TIME!"
