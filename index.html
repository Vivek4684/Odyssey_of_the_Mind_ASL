Story Summary

Lost in Omerland — Division 2, Problem 2

Capt. SUPER and DUPER set out from planet CARNIVOSIA to find a mythical crystal to save their dying planet. After a meteor crash-lands them on the wrong spot, they meet DR. BLOOPNAR and the quirky device SIMETRA.

Their journey takes them through Cyberpunk City where they befriend a robot, survive a betrayal in a glowing forest, and discover the Crystal Cave. In the end, they find the crystal was buried under their crashed UFO all along!

The story celebrates friendship, forgiveness, and teamwork.
